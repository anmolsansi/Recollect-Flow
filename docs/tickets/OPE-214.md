## Current free-tier behavior

- Workers Free includes 100,000 dynamic requests per day.
- D1 Free includes 5 million rows read per day, 100,000 rows written per day,
  and 5 GB total storage. Daily limits reset at 00:00 UTC and operations fail
  until reset when a daily limit is exhausted.
- Workers AI includes 10,000 neurons per day. The allocation resets at 00:00
  UTC and further operations fail after exhaustion.
- R2 Standard includes 10 GB-month of storage, 1 million Class A operations,
  and 10 million Class B operations per month. The free allocation does not
  apply to Infrequent Access storage.
