## Current free-tier behavior

- Workers Free includes 100,000 dynamic requests per day.
- D1 Free includes 5 million rows read per day, 100,000 rows written per day,
  and 5 GB total storage. Daily limits reset at 00:00 UTC and operations fail
  until reset when a daily limit is exhausted.

  UTC and further operations fail after exhaustion.
- R2 Standard includes 10 GB-month of storage, 1 million Class A operations,

## Spending-control verification

The production resources remain within free allocations. The 2026-07-19
read-only snapshot reported D1 at 131 KB with 73 rows read and 53 rows written
over 24 hours, and R2 at zero objects/zero bytes. This setup did not add a paid
subscription or payment method.

Cloudflare budget alerts are an account-spend feature for pay-as-you-go
customers. The current OAuth grant does not include alert-policy permission, so
no alert policy is claimed here. The release control is therefore:

- remain on free allocations with provider hard limits;
- keep application warnings at 70% and 90% and stop optional work at the hard
  threshold; and
- before any pay-as-you-go enablement, require owner approval and create an
  account-level dollar budget alert in the Cloudflare dashboard.
